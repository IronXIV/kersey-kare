Considering a career with kersey kare limited?

Here at kersey kare limited, we are constantly on the lookout for new recruits to fill care positions across Nottinghamshire and our other service areas. Our working hours are flexible and can be personally tailored to suit you. We strive to place you in positions that are both varied and rewarding.

We offer excellent rates of pay.

  • We are able to provide an unlimited number of shifts so you can take on as little or as much work as you want.
  • We will ensure you receive enthusiastic and encouraging support from our dedicated management team.
  • We offer flexible shift patterns and working hours to fit around your needs, including night shifts and part-time positions.
  • You will have plenty of opportunities to progress your career in rewarding and diverse roles.
    • We can provide both long term and short term temporary placements depending on your requirements..

    Our Service Standards

    Our kersey kare limited head office is home to a dedicated recruitment and vetting team who ensure that every applicant goes through at least 20 different checks before they are hired. This means that only the highest calibre of staff makes it into our team.
